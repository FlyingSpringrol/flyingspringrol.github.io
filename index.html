April 2016-June 2016

Finished up community college, and started working on a project that I'd been meaning to do for a while. Me and some friends were trying to better visualize biology using Virtual Reality technology. I went and found an Oculus, then made and textured the 3D models of cells before putting them into Unity. It was pretty cool, and I still think to this day that the best use case of VR is education. It took us about 2 months to put it all together though, and this incredible difficulty led us to think up a piece of software to make the process easier. We wanted to compress the 3d creation pipeline (3d modeling [in Blender] -> Texturing [photoshop/Quixel] -> Rendering Engine [Unity]), into a single application. So we set out to build that next.
