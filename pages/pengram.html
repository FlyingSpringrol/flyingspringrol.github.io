VR design work

I do design work for people who ask. Creating solutions to very specific VR problems is one of my favorite things to do.
If you have any VR design stuff, hmu. I'll do it for super cheap/free, because it really doesn't feel like work for me. It's just so damn fun creating tools that people have never seen or used before, but still have recognizable design affordances and signifiers attached to them. It's challenging, but it's an exercise that stretches artistic and technical muscles in equal ways.
